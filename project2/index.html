How to Play

Remember the emoji sequence that will be flashed on the screen, and input the correct combination to win the round. You only get one shot for each round, so Focus! Each correct answer will win you points. The higher the level of difficulty, the more items there will be in the sequence, and the more points you can earn. The goal of the game is to beat your highest score. Yes! You are your own Archenemy!


Good luck and enjoy!
