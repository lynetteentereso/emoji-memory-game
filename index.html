How to Play

Remember the emoji sequence that will be flashed on the screen and input the correct combination to win the round. You only get one shot for each round. Each correct answer will win you points. Each level of difficulty will have more items in the sequence and will give you higher points. The goal of the game is to beat your highest score. Yes! You are your own Archnemesis!


Good luck and enjoy!
